Let your visitors show their support by planting a tree in your Ecologi forest.

== Description ==
Buy me a tree is a simple plugin that displays a link to your <a href="https://ecologi.com/">Ecologi forest</a> on your website.

This plugin is in its early stages, feedback and suggestions are greatly welcomed.

Please note: Buy me a Tree is not owned by Ecologi and does not act on its behalf.

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= Who are Ecologi? =

If you haven't heard of Ecologi, you should definitely <a href="https://ecologi.com/"> check them out!</a> 

Ecologi is a platform for real climate action. They facilitate the funding of carbon offset projects and tree planting around the world. 

Their mission is to remove 50% of global CO2 emissions by 2040 and responsibly plant billions of trees every year. 

= Do I need an Ecologi account? =
Not quite.

Having an Ecologi account is a great way to fund tree planting and Gold Standard carbon offsetting, it's also a great way to see your and your supporters contribution as your forest grows.

If you would like to use your account, but haven't yet got one, you can sign up through <a href="https://ecologi.com/buymeatree?r=6082aabfc20ae9d38b09d29d">this referral link</a> to immediately recieve 30 trees in your forest.

Not quite ready to create an account? That's not a problem, you can ask a friend to use theirs, or simply the developers - https://ecologi.com/buymeatree?r=6082aabfc20ae9d38b09d29d.
